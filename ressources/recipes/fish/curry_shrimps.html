Curry Shrimps

4 20 min 20 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
500 g Shrimp
300 g Rice
250 ml Coconut milk
250 g Snow peas
30 g Coriander
5 Garlic clove
2 Carrot
2 Onion
2 Bell pepper
0.5 Mango
Curry
Cayenne pepper

Step 1

Defrost, shell the shrimps. Slice the carrots and bell pepper. Dice the mango.

Step 2

In a large pan, brown the shrimps in preheated oil. Add coriander, sliced onions. Cook for approximately 2 minutes (not any longer !).

Step 3

Add the carrots, chopped snow peas, salt, pepper, curry to taste. Take shrimps over high heat. Lower heat. Cook for 8 minutes

Step 4

Add the diced mango, garlic and then coconut milk. Simmer over low heat for 10 minutes.
