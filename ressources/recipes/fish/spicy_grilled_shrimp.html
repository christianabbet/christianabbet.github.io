Spicy Grilled Shrimp

4 20 min 5 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
800 g Shrimp
2 tbsp Olive oil
0.5 tbsp Salt
2 tsp Lemon juice
1 tsp Paprika
0.5 tsp Cayenne pepper
2 Garlic clove

Step 1

In a small bowl, crush the garlic with the salt. Mix in cayenne pepper and paprika, and then stir in olive oil and lemon juice to form a paste.

Step 2

In a large bowl, toss shrimp with garlic paste until evenly coated.

Step 3

Lightly oil grill grate. Cook shrimp for 2 to 3 minutes per side, or until opaque.
