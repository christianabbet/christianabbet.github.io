Shrimps Stew

4 20 min 40 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
800 g Shrimp
20 g Butter
40 cl Water
20 cl Crème fraîche
10 cl White wine
3 Tomato
2 Carrot
1 Onion
1 Lime
1 tbsp Cornstarch
Parsley
Chive

Step 1

Shell srimps. Sauté shrimps and shells in butter. Add white wine. Bring to a boil and add water, parsley, chive and chopped onion.

Step 2

Simmer for 20 minutes.

Step 3

Meanwhile, peal and cut into sticks carrots. Dice tomatoes.

Step 4

Filter the aroma and bring it to a boil. Add carrots and tomatoes. Cook for 15 minutes. Season.

Step 5

Mix crème fraîche and cornstarch together and pour in the aroma. Stir until it thickens,

Step 6

Add shrimps and lime juice. Let heat up.
