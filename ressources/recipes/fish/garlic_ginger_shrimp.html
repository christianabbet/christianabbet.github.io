Garlic Ginger Shrimp

4 5 min 10 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
500 g Shrimp
2 tbsp Fish sauce
1 tbsp Soy sauce
2 tsp Cornstarch
2 tsp Ginger
1 tsp Cilantro
1 Green onion
1 Garlic cloves

Step 1

In a small bowl, combine fish sauce, soy sauce and cilantro and set aside.

Step 2

Pat the shrimp very dry with paper towels. In a medium bowl, add shrimp and cornstarch and toss to coat.

Step 3

In a wok pan over high heat, add half the cooking oil. When the wok is very hot, add the shrimp in a single layer and cook partially until one side is seared brown, about 1 minute.

Step 4

Flip and sear the other side of each shrimp, about one more minute. They don't need to be cooked all the way through yet. Remove them to a plate or bowl and set aside.

Step 5

Turn the heat down to medium and let wok cool off a bit to prevent the aromatics from burning. Add the remaining cooking oil and add green onion, garlic and ginger and stir fry for a minute until fragrant.

Step 6

Pour in the sauce mixture and add the shrimp back into the pan. Stir fry for another minute until shrimp is cooked through.
