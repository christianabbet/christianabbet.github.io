Stuffed Calamari

6 1.5 h 1 h

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
6 Calamari
60 g Rice
30 g Parsley
3 Tomato
2 Onion
2 Egg yolk
Cayenne pepper

Step 1

Detach head from camalri body (with one quick pull). Remove cartilage and ink bag. Remove fins. Separate tentacle from head (be careful to remove the cartilage ball). Wash, drain. Put mantle, tentacles and fins aside.

Step 2

Wash the rice, put it in a saucepan and add water and salt. Cook for 10 minutes then drain. Set aside.

Step 3

Chop the tentacles and fins. Brown them in olive oil with one finely chopped onion and one diced tomato.

Step 4

Add salt, pepper, cayenne pepper, cooked rice and finely chopped parsley. Stir. Add a bit of water and cook over medium heat during 15 minutes. Add eggs yolk. Stir. Set aside.

Step 5

Fill up mantle to three quarters with filling. Put all calamari standing in a large casserole. Close the mantle using a wire or toothpicks.

Step 6

Add some oil and cook the calamari over medium heat. Add salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, one chopped onion, two diced tomatoes. Cook for 15 minutes. Be careful and add some water if needed.
