Apple Fritters

4 20 min 10 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
3 Apple
300 g Flour
250 ml Milk
1 tbsp Kirsh
2 Egg
10 g Vanilla sugar
5 g Dry yeast
Sugar

Step 1

Mix flour, yeast, vanilla sugar and egg yolk. Slowly add milk and Kirsh while stirring. Take egg white up in snow. Add a pinch of salt. Slowly add it to the egg yolks mixture

Step 2

Peal, seed and slice apples.

Step 3

Heat oil in a pan. Soak apple slices in the mixture and fry them in oil on both sides.

Step 4

Set aside on paper towel and let drain. Sprinkle with sugar.
