Italian Bruschetta

5 30 min -

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
1 Bread
500 g Tomato
100 g Parmesan
5 g Basil
3 tbsp Olive oil
2 tbsp Balsamic vinegar
1 tsp Pepper
0.5 tsp Salt
2 Garlic clove

Step 1

In a large bowl put diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, parmesan, salt and pepper.

Step 2

Carefully mix and let rest for 15 minutes.

Step 3

Serve on grilled bread slices.
