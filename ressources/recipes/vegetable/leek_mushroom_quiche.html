Leek Mushroom Quiche

6 30 min 30 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
150 g Mushroom
100 g Parmesan
10 g Butter
2 dl Cream
3 Egg
1 Shortcrust pastry
1 Leek
1 tsp Nutmeg

Step 1

Preheat oven at 180°C.

Step 2

Wash and chop leek. Sauté leek in a pan with butter during 5 minutes.

Step 3

Wash mushrooms and chop them. Sauté mushroom in a pan with olive oil during 5 minutes

Step 4

Whip eggs, cream and spices. Add parmesan and whip again.

Step 5

Put pastry in a mold and spread mushrooms and leek over it. Pour the egg/parmesan mixture over it. Put in oven for 30 minutes.
