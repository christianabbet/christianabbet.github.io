Peanut Butter Chicken

6 20 min 10 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
700 g Chicken
500 ml Coconut milk
250 g Snow peas
200 ml Chicken stock
80 g Peanut butter
40 g Salt-free Penut
20 g Flour
1 Garlic clove
1 Onion
2 tbsp Sugar
1 tbsp Red curry paste
Peanut oil

Step 1

Grind half of the peanuts and mix with flour.

Step 2

Thinly slice chicken and coat them with flour mixture.

Step 3

In a wok, heat peanut oil. Brown the meat thoroughly during 5 minutes. Set aside.

Step 4

Add some peanut oil in the wok and lightly brown garlic and chopped onion.

Step 5

Add snow peas. Add coconut milk, sugar, curry paste and finally peanut butter.

Step 6

Mix, then add chicken. Simmer for 10 minutes. If the sauce is too thick add chicken stock. Add salt, pepper.
