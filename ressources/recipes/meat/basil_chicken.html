Basil Chicken

3 15 min 10 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
3 Chicken breast
100 g Bean sprouts
25 g Basil
1 Onion
1 Green bell pepper
200 ml Chicken stock
2 tbsp Soy sauce
1 tbsp Cornstarch
Withe pepper
Cayenne pepper

Step 1

Chop the onion and thinly slice the bell pepper.

Step 2

Slice into thin slices the chicken breast using a sharp knife. Heat some oil in a large pan.

Step 3

Put the chicken in the pan and stir during one minute. Chicken should not be cooked, just slightly brown.

Step 4

Add the soy sauce, white pepper and cayenne pepper. Add chopped onion, sliced bell pepper and bean sprouts. Mix vigorously over high heat.

Step 5

Add basil and chicken stock.

Step 6

Add the cornstarch. Stir. When it starts getting thick and syrupy remove form the heat.
