Sweet-and-Sour Chicken

2 10 min 10 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
2 Chicken breast
2 Onion
2 Carrot
1 Baby pineapple
1 Red bell pepper
1 l Vegetable stock
3 tbsp Rice vinegar
2 tbsp Ketchup
2 tbsp Fish sauce
1 tbsp Mirin
1 tbsp Cornstarch
2 tsp Sugar
2 tsp Brown sugar

Step 1

Dice chicken breast. Sauté chicken in a large pan with olive oil during 3 to 4 minutes. Set aside

Step 2

Sauté vegetables and pineapple. Add vegetable stock.

Step 3

Bring to a boil and add all ingredient except cornstarch and chicken.

Step 4

Let cook until sauce thickens then add chicken. Add cornstarch and season. Let reduce.
