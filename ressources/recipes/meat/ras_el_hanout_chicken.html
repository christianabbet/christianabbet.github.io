Ras el Hanout Chicken

4 20 min 45 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
80 g Peas
1 dl Chicken stock
4 Chicken wings
4 Garlic clove
2 Zucchini
2 Carrot
2 Onion
4 tbsp Ras-el-hanout

Step 1

Coat chicken with ras-el-hanout. Heat oil in a casserole. Add chicken and stir until it browns. Add minced onions, garlic and chicken stock. Lower heat. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes.

Step 2

Add slat and sliced carrots. Simmer over low heat until carrots are almost cooked.

Step 3

Add peas, some water. Rise the temperature and bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer over low heat for 10 minutes.
