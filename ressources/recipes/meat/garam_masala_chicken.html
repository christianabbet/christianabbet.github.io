Garam Masala Chicken

4 20 min 10 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
4 Chicken breast
2 Tomato
1 Onion
1 Garlic clove
60 ml Water
1 tbsp Olive oil
2 tsp Garam masala

Step 1

Heat oil in skillet on medium-high heat. Cook chicken 3 minutes or until lightly browned. Turn chicken. Add chopped onion. Cook and stir for 2 more minutes.

Step 2

Stir in diced tomatoes, garlic salt and water. Sprinkle 1 1/2 teaspoons of the Garam Masala over chicken and vegetables. Bring to boil.

Step 3

Reduce heat,cover and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally or until chicken and vegetables are tender.

Step 4

Stir remaining 1/2 teaspoon Garam Masala into skillet. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Cook 5 minutes longer, stirring occasionally.
