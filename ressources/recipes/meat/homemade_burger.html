Homemade Burger

4 20 min 35 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
100 g Bacon
50 g Mayonnaise
50 g Ketchup
50 g Salad rocket
40 ml Oil
30 ml Balsamic vinegar
4 Ground meat patty
4 Burger bread
3 Onion
2 Garlic clove
2 Avocado
1 Tomato
1 Chili pepper
1 Lime
0.5 tbsp Honey
0.25 tsp Salt

Step 1

In a bowl, mix mayonnaise, ketchup, chopped half onion, one chopped garlic clove, black pepper and chopped chili pepper.

Step 2

In a bowl, grind chopped half onion, one chopped garlic colve, avocado and lime juice. Season.

Step 3

In a sauce pan, caramelize the two left onions in oil during 15 to 20 minutes.

Step 4

Add balsamic vinegar, honey, salt. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes until it thickens. Season.

Step 5

In a fry pan, cook meat and fry bacon.

Step 6

Assemble burger. Start with bread, then gravy, meat, rocket salad, sliced tomato, caramelize onion, bacon, bread.
