Maple Syrup Chicken

4 20 min 25 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
500 g Chicken breast
340 g Vegetable oil
250 ml Maple syrup
140 g Flour
4 Garlic clove
2 Egg
2 tbsp Soy sauce
1 tbsp Cornstarch
1 tsp Dry thyme
1 tsp Dry oregano
0.5 tsp Paprika
0.25 tsp Cayenne pepper

Step 1

In a saucepan mix maple syrup, garlic and soy sauce over medium heat. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch, 4 tbsp of water. Add it to maple syrup and stir until it thickens (about 1 to 2 minutes). Set aside.

Step 2

Heat vegetable oil in a pan. In a bowl mix flour, thyme, oregano, paprika and cayenne pepper.

Step 3

Cut chicken breast into strips. Season with salt, pepper.

Step 4

Coat each chicken piece with flour, then beaten eggs, then flour again. Hardly press to allow flour to stick properly.

Step 5

Fry the chicken slices in hot oil. Remove from oil when it browns (about 3-4 minute on each side). Put them on a plat covered with paper towels so oil will be drained.

Step 6

Serve with maple syrup dip.
