Stuffed Eggplants

4 25 min 45 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
300 g Minced meat
5 Garlic clove
4 Eggplant
3 Tomato
1 Onion
Basil
Mozzarella

Step 1

Preheat oven at 180Â°C.

Step 2

Cut in half the eggplants longitudinally. Cut squares without damaging the eggplant skin. Put in the oven and pour 1 tbsp of olive oil on top.

Step 3

Peel, seed and dice the tomatoes. Peel and chop the garlic and onion.

Step 4

Cook the meat for 4 to 5 minutes. Add onion and garlic. Add salt, pepper and cook for 5 minutes. Add diced tomatoes, stir for 10 minutes. Stop the cooking and insert basil.

Step 5

Take eggplants out of the oven, empty them out taking care to not damage the skin. Set skins aside. Mix meat with eggplants.

Step 6

Stuff each skin with the mixture. Add mozzarella on top and put in the oven during 20 minutes.
