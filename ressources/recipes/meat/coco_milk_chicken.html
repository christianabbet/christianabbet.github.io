Coco Milk Chicken

4 20 min 1 h

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
700 g Chicken
500 ml Coconut milk
400 ml Tomate paste
2 Onion
1 Bell pepper
Cayenne pepper

Step 1

In a casserole, brown diced chicken with olive oil. Add salt, pepper, cayenne pepper.

Step 2

Add water and simmer for 30 minutes.

Step 3

Mince the onion and slice bell pepper. Pour in the casserole and add coconut milk, then tomato paste.

Step 4

Reduce for about 30 minutes
