Indian Chicken

3 15 min 30 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
250 ml Coconut milk
150 g Basmati rice
4 Chicken breast
3 Garlic clove
1 Tomato
1 Onion
1 tbsp Ginger
1 tsp Cumin
1 tsp Paprika
0.5 tsp Turmeric
0.5 tsp Cinnamon
0.25 tsp Cardamom
Black peppercorn
Olive oil

Step 1

Pour rice in a saucepan, add water, olive oil, salt, black peppercorn and cardamom. Simmer for 10 minutes over low heat and cover. Set aside.

Step 2

Peel, mince onion and garlic. Mix them with ginger, add some water and mash.

Step 3

In a pan heat the mash with cumin, cinnamon, turmeric and paprika. Stir. Add coconut milk and simmer over medium heat.

Step 4

Dice chicken and brown them in a pan with olive oil. Add diced tomatoes and salt.

Step 5

When the chicken is cooked, mix it with coconut milk. Serve with rice.
