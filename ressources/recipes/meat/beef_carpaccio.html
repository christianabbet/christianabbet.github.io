Beef Carpaccio

2 15 min -

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
8 Toasts
200 g Beef tenderloin
250 ml Arugula salad
100 g Parmesan
1 Egg yolk
3 tbsp Olive oil
1 tbsp Balsamic vinegar
1 tsp Mustard
0.5 tsp Garlic
0.25 tsp Sugar

Step 1

Place beef tenderloin in the freezer until it partially freeze.

Step 2

In a bowl mix chopped garlic, mustard, egg yolk, sugar and vinegar.

Step 3

Add olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 4

Using a sharp knife, cut thin beef slices. Spread slices over a plate. Spread arugula salad over it. Pour oil mixture over the meat and sprinkle chopped parmesan.

Step 5

Serve with grilled toasts and butter.
