Mexican Chicken and Lentils

3 15 min 30 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
4 Chicken breast
200 g Lentil
30 g Coriander
3 Garlic clove
1 Avocado
1 Onion
1 Tomato
3 tbsp Sour cream
0.75 tbsp Cumin
0.75 tbsp Chili powder

Step 1

Preheat oven at 180Â°C.

Step 2

In a large bowl, mix cumin, chili powder, salt, 1 tbsp of oil and chicken breasts. Put the chicken breasts in oven.

Step 3

Bake for 25 minutes.

Step 4

Meanwhile, wash lentils. Put them in a saucepan with 3 cup of water. Simmer during 25 minutes, drain. Set aside.

Step 5

In a pan brown minced onion. Add salt, pepper. Add garlic, sliced avocado, diced tomato, sour cream and lentils. Stir.

Step 6

Before serving add coriander and slice chicken breast.
