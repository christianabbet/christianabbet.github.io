Spicy Chicken Soup

5 15 min 1.5 h

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
1 l Chicken broth
400 g Chicken breast
400 g Chili beans
400 g Peeled tomatoes
200 g Chunky salsa
200 g Whole kernel corn
100 g Sour cream
2 Garlic clove
1 Onion
2 tbsp Olive oil
1.5 tbsp Chili powder
1 tbsp Dried parsley
0.5 tbsp Onion powder
0.5 tsp Black pepper
0.5 tsp Garlic powder
0.25 tsp Salt

Step 1

In a large pot over medium heat, combine chicken broth, chicken, salt, pepper, garlic powder, parsley and onion powder.

Step 2

Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 1 hour, or until chicken juices run clear. Remove chicken, reserve broth. Shred chicken.

Step 3

In a large pot over medium heat, cook onion and garlic in olive oil until slightly browned.

Step 4

Stir in salsa, peeled tomatoes, chili powder, corn, chili beans, sour cream, shredded chicken and half of the broth. Simmer 30 minutes.
