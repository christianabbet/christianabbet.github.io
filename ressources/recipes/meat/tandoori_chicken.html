Tandoori Chicken

4 20 min 25 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
900 g Chicken
300 ml Plain yogurt
1 tsp Salt
1 Lemon
0.5 Onion
1 Garlic clove
2 tsp Garam Masala
2 tsp Cilantro
1 tsp Ginger
1 tsp Cayenne pepper
1 tsp Yellow food coloring
1 tsp Red food coloring

Step 1

Remove skin from chicken pieces, and cut slits into them lengthwise. Place in a shallow dish. Sprinkle both sides of chicken with salt and lemon juice. Set aside 20 minutes.

Step 2

In a medium bowl, combine yogurt, onion, garlic, ginger, garam masala, and cayenne pepper. Stir in yellow and red food coloring. Spread yogurt mixture over chicken. Cover, and refrigerate for 12 hours.

Step 3

Cook chicken in a large pan over medium heat. Cook chicken until no longer pink and juices run clear.
