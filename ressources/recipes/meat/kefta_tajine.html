Kefta Tajine

5 15 min 25 min

Ingredients

Sauce

Quantity Name
300 g Crushed tomatoes
7 g Coriander
5 Egg
1 Onion
1 Garlic clove
1 tsp Cumin
1 tsp Paprika
1 tsp Ginger

Kefta

Quantity Name
1 Garlic clove
7 g Coriander
500 g Minced meat
1 Onion
1 tsp Paprika
1 tsp Cumin
1 tsp Curcuma
1 tsp Salt

Step 1

Chop onion and garlic. In a tajine or a large pan, pour olive oil, onion, garlic, coriander, tomatoes and spices. Simmer over low heat for 5 minutes.

Step 2

In a large bowl, mix together, oignon, garlic, coriander and spices. Form meatballs. Spread meatballs over the sauce. Simmer for 5 minutes.

Step 3

Carefully break eggs over the meatballs. Cover and simmer until the egg white is cooked but the egg yolk remains free-flowing.
