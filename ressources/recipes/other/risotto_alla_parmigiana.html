Risotto Alla Parmigiana

4 5 min 25 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
750 ml Vegetable stock
300 g Risotto rice
100 ml White wine
50 g Parmesan
20 g Butter
0.5 White onion
1 tbsp Cream

Step 1

Heat oil in a skillet pan. Stir in chopped onion and saut√© them.

Step 2

Add rice and stir until rice becomes slightly transparent.

Step 3

Stir in white wine and let evaporate.

Step 4

Bring vegetable stock to a boil. Progressively stir in stock. When done, remove from heat.

Step 5

Add grated parmesan and butter. Season. Add cream and stir for a while.
