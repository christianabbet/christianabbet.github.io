Italian Pesto

4 15 min -

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
150 g Parmesan
60 g Basil
30 g Pine nut
3 Garlic clove
Olive oil

Step 1

In a grinder pour one third of the parmesan, basil, pine nuts and garlic. Grind.

Step 2

Add olive oil then stir. Add oil until the mixture become almost liquid. Set aside

Step 3

Do it again with the rest of the ingredient (2 more times).

Step 4

A the bottom of a jar put some olive oil. Then add the pesto sauce. Before closing the jar add olive oil on top.
