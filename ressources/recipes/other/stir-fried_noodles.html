Stir-Fried Noodles

3 15 min 10 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
150 g Egg noodle
100 g Mangetout peas
15 g Ginger
2 tbsp Soy sauce
1 tbsp Vegetable oil
1 tsp Wine vinegar
2 Garlic clove
2 Scallion
1 Carrot
1 Yellow bell pepper

Step 1

Cook the noodles according to pack instructions.

Step 2

Meanwhile, heat the oil in a wok or large frying pan, then stir-fry the ginger, garlic, sliced carrot, sliced pepper and mangetout over a high heat for 2-3 mins.

Step 3

Drain the noodles thoroughly, add to the pan with the onions then stir-fry for 2 mins.

Step 4

Mix together the soy sauce and vinegar, stir into the pan, then cook for 1-2 mins.
