Homemade French Fries

4 25 min 30 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
1 kg Potato
100 g Butter
1 Garlic clove
0.5 Shallot
1 tsp Parsley
Tabasco
Lemon juice
Salt

Step 1

Preheat oven at 220Â°C.

Step 2

Peal and thinly slice potatoes. Pour potatoes in cold water for 10 minutes.

Step 3

In a bowl, mix butter, garlic, shallot, parsley, a drop of tabasco and a drop of lemon juice.

Step 4

In a large bowl, mix sliced potatoes and butter. Put in oven for 30 minutes. Add salt before serving.
