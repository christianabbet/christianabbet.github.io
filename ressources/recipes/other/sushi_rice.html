Sushi Rice

8 20 min 20 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
300 g Short grain rice
300 ml Water
4 tbsp Rice vinegar
2 tbsp Sugar
0.5 tbsp Salt

Step 1

Wash rice in a sieve with cold water. Continue while water is not clear. Drain and let rest for 30 minutes.

Step 2

Pour rice and water in a saucepan. Cover and bring to a boil over medium heat. Trust your sense of hearing. Do not remove the cover. Let cook for 5 more minutes.

Step 3

Lower heat and let cook for 10 minutes. Remove cover and let cool for 10 minutes.

Step 4

In a saucepan heat rice vinegar, salt, sugar, without bringing it to a boil. When sugar is completely dissolved, allow to cool.

Step 5

Pour rice in a large bowl. Pour rice vinegar mixture, while carefully stirring to not crush rice grains.
