Cherry Tomato Pasta

4 5 min 15 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
500 g Cherry tomato
400 g Linguine pasta
20 g Basil
125 ml Olive oil
1 tsp Salt
0.75 tsp Sugar
0.5 tsp Pepper
2 Garlic cloves
Parmesan

Step 1

Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water, stirring occasionally, until al dente; drain and transfer to a large bowl.

Step 2

Meanwhile, heat oil in a skillet over medium-high. Add garlic, then tomatoes, pepper, sugar, and salt.

Step 3

Cook, stirring occasionally, until tomatoes burst and release their juices to form a sauce, 6â€“8 minutes.

Step 4

Toss pasta with tomato sauce and basil. Top with Parmesan.
