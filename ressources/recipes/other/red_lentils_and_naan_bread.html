Red lentils and Naan Bread

10 30 min 3 h

Ingredients

Naan Bread

Quantity Name
630 g Flour
240 ml Water
50 g Sugar
55 g Butter
7 g Dry Yeast
3 Garlic clove
1 Egg
3 tbsp Milk
2 tsp Salt

Main Dish

Quantity Name
300 g Potato
200 g Red lentil
20 g Ginger
1 l Vegetable stock
6 Scallion
2 Garlic clove
4 Carrot
3 tbsp Vegtable oil
1 tbsp Curry
0.75 tsp Salt
0.25 tsp Pepper

Step 1

In a large bowl, mix flour and dry yeast. Stir in sugar, milk, beaten egg and salt Knead for 6 to 8 minutes on a lightly floured surface, or until smooth. Place dough in a well oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and set aside to rise. Let it rise 1 hour.

Step 2

Punch down dough, and knead in garlic. Pinch off small handfuls of dough about the size of a golf ball. Roll into balls, and place on a tray. Cover with a towel, and allow to rise until doubled in size, about 30 minutes.

Step 3

During the second rising, preheat oven at 220Â°C and set to grill mode.

Step 4

Roll one ball of dough out into a thin circle. Lightly oil dough bottom. Place dough in oven, and cook for 4 to 5 minutes, or until puffy and lightly browned. Brush cooked side with butter, and turn over. Brush uncooked side with butter, and cook until browned, another 4 to 5 minutes. Remove from oven, and continue the process until all the naan has been prepared.

Step 5

Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add the ginger, garlic, and scallion whites and cook, stirring frequently, until softened, 2 to 3 minutes.

Step 6

Stir in the curry powder. Add the sliced carrots, diced potato, lentils, broth, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer, stirring occasionally, until the lentils and vegetables are tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
