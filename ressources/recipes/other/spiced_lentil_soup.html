Spiced Lentil Soup

4 10 min 15 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
1 l Vegetable stock
600 g Carrot
140 g Red lentil
125 ml Milk
2 tbsp Olive oil
2 tsp Cumin seeds
0.5 tsp Cayenne pepper
Plain yogurt

Step 1

Heat a large saucepan and dry-fry the cumin seeds until they start to jump around the pan and release their aromas. Scoop out about half of the seeds with a spoon and set aside.

Step 2

Add the oil, coarsely grated carrot, lentils, stock and milk to the pan and bring to the boil. Simmer for 15 mins until the lentils have swollen and softened.

Step 3

Whizz the soup with a stick blender or in a food processor until smooth. Season to taste and finish with a dollop of yogurt and a sprinkling of the reserved toasted spices.
