Tomato-Cream Sauce

5 5 min 15 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
400 g Tomato
120 ml Heavy cream
1 Onion
1 Garlic clove
2 tbsp Olive oil
1 tbsp Basil
1 tbsp Butter
0.75 tsp Sugar
0.25 tsp Oregano
0.25 tsp Salt
0.125 tsp Pepper

Step 1

In a saucepan, saute chopped onion and garlic in olive oil over medium heat. Make sure it doesn't burn. Add diced tomatoes, basil, sugar, oregano, salt and pepper.

Step 2

Bring to boil and continue to boil 5 minutes or until most of the liquid evaporates. Remove from heat; stir in whipping cream and butter. Reduce heat and simmer 5 more minutes.
