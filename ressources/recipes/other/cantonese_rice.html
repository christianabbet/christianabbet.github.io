Cantonese Rice

4 10 min 12 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
600 ml Water
300 g Basmati rice
150 g Peas
150 g Ham
1 dl Oil
2 tbsp Chives
1 tbsp Parsley
2 Garlic clove
2 Egg
1 Onion

Step 1

In a sauce pan heat oil. Saut√© garlic and chopped onion. Add rice and let fry a bit.

Step 2

Add peas, ham. Pour hot water. Season. Stir. Cover and cook for 12 minutes over low heat.

Step 3

Meanwhile in another pan add eggs. Scramble.

Step 4

Add scrambled eggs. Stir. Sprinkle the chopped chives and parsley
