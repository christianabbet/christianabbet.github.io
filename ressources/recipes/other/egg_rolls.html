Egg rolls

10 1 h 30 min

Ingredients

Egg roll

Quantity Name
250 g Minced pork meat
100 g Shrimp
8 Sheets brick pastry
3 Egg
2 Garlic clove
1 Onion
1 Carrot
150 g Chinese mushroom
50 g Rice vermicelli
50 g Bean sprouts
4 tbsp Fish sauce

Sauce

Quantity Name
3 tbsp Fish sauce
3 tbsp Rice vinegar
3 tbsp Sugar
4 Garlic clove
2 Red pepper
1.5 dl Water

Step 1

Soak mushroom in cold water for 1h. Soak rice vermicelli in cold water for 10 minutes.

Step 2

Chop and mix pork, shrimps, onion, carrot, mushrooms, rice vermicelli, bean sprouts and eggs. Add black pepper and fish sauce.

Step 3

On a brick pastry sheet, spread some meat mixture and roll.

Step 4

In a saucepan, heat oil and add garlic cloves. Fry, over medium heat, the egg rolls.

Step 5

In a saucepan, heat sugar, water, fish sauce, vinegar. Add chopped garlic and minced red pepper. Allow to cool.
