Mushrooms Vol-au-Vent

8 40 min 12 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
250 g Mushroom
60 g Ham
60 g Flour
60 g Butter
1 l Milk
8 Vol-au-vent

Step 1

Preheat oven at 180°C.

Step 2

Chop ham and mushrooms. In a pan add oil and sauté mushrooms and ham.

Step 3

In a saucepan melt butter, slowly add flour while stirring to avoid lumps. Add milk and stir until it thickens. Season. Add mushrooms and ham. Stir. Simmer for 10 minutes

Step 4

Fill vol-au-vent with sauce. Put vol-au-vent in oven in 180°C for 15 minutes
