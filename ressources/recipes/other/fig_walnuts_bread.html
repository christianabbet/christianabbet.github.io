Fig Walnuts Bread

12 3 h 1 h

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
500 g Whole-wheat flour
300 ml Water
100 g Walnuts
100 g Fig
60 g Raisins
1 tbsp Oil
1 tsp Sucre
1 tsp Salt
7 g Dry yeast

Step 1

Mix flour with dry yeast. Add sugar, salt, walnuts, figs and raisins.

Step 2

Add tepid water, a few at a time, while stirring by hand until dough becomes a bit sticky. If dough is too sticky add flour.

Step 3

Add oil and knead during 10 minutes

Step 4

Let rest the dough in a large bowl for 1 hour and a half (1 hour at least). Cover with a linen.

Step 5

Knead the dough for 10 minutes and add flour so the dough will not stick anymore.

Step 6

Form a ball. Make a cut at the top of it. Put in oven in 150Â°C for 1 hour.
