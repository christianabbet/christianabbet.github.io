Chestnuts Risotto

4 10 min 30 min

Ingredients

Risotto

Quantity Name
300 g Risotto rice
150 g Chestnut
9 dl Beef stock
1 dl White wine
2 Garlic clove
1 Onion

Sauce

Quantity Name
75 g Mascarpone
60 g Bacon
50 g Parmesan
2 tbsp Butter
2 tbsp Hazelnut
30 g Sage

Step 1

In a large pan add butter and saut√© onion and garlic. Add rice and wine. Simmer until wine is completely absorbed.

Step 2

Gradually pour beef stock, taking care rice is always covered by water. Simmer for 20 minutes. Add chesnuts during the last 10 minutes.

Step 3

Roast hazelnuts in a pan. Grind. Set aside. Brown bacon, add butter and roasted hazelnuts.

Step 4

Gradually add mascarpone and parmesan to rice. Season.

Step 5

When serving, spread bacon and sage on top
