Spiced Curry Rice

4 10 min 30 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
300 g Basmati rice
25 g Raisins
25 g Slivered almonds
25 g Butter
15 g Coriander
1 l Chicken stock
100 ml Red curry paste
1 Onion
1 tsp Turmeric
0.5 tsp Cinnamon
0.5 tsp Cardamom

Step 1

Fry the onions and whole spices in the butter for 10 mins. Add the curry paste and cook until aromatic.

Step 2

Add the rice and the raisins, stir and cook for a good few mins, then add the stock and bring to the boil.

Step 3

Cover with a tight-fitting lid and when really boiling lower the heat and cook for 10 mins.

Step 4

Turn the heat off but donâ€™t lift the lid and leave to sit for 20 mins.

Step 5

Stir in half the toasted almonds and chopped coriander, with the rest of the coriander and the almonds thrown over the top.
