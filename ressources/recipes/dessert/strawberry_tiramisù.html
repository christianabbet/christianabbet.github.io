Strawberry Tiramisù

6 30 min -

Ingredients

Tiramisù

Quantity Name
500 g Strawberries
500 g Mascarpone
90 g Icing sugar
10 g Vanilla sugar
50 ml Limoncello
12 Ladyfingers
3 Egg

Step 1

Wash, top and tail the strawberries.

Step 2

In a bowl, crush 3-4 strawberries with the limoncello and 2 tsp of sugar. Set aside.

Step 3

Cut the other strawberries in half

Step 4

Separate the yolks from the whites. Whip the yolks withe the sugar. Add the mascarpone, vanilla sugar and mix until the mixture becomes smooth and soft.

Step 5

In a bowl, take up whites in snow.

Step 6

Add the whites to the mascarpone. Arrange the ladyfingers on the bottom of a mold. Glaze them with the limoncello grout.

Step 7

Pour half of the mascarpone over the biscuits. Add strawberries, then add mascarpone again. Put in the fridge for 3 hours.
