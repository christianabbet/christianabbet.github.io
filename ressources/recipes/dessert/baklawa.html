Baklawa

20 50 min 20 min

Ingredients

Syrup

Quantity Name
300 g Sugar
1 dl Water
1 tbsp Water of orange flowers
1 tsp Lemon juice

Filling

Quantity Name
500 g Filo pastry
200 g Almonds
180 g Butter
100 g Walnuts
50 g Pistachio
3 tbsp Icing sugar
1 tbsp Water of orange flowers

Step 1

Pour sugar, water, water of orange flower and lemon juice in a saucepan. Heat mixture until it becomes syrupy. Set aside.

Step 2

Preheat oven at 180Â°C. Grill almonds, walnuts and pistachio. Coarsely chop them. Add icing sugar and wator of orange flowers. Mix well.

Step 3

Melt butter. Butter a baking pan. Spread one sheet of filo pastry and butter it. Add a second sheet and butter it as well. Continue until half of the filo sheets remains.

Step 4

Pour nuts mixture and flatten. Spread the remaining filo sheets on top. Butter each filo sheet. Cut baklawas, using a sharp knife, forming squares.

Step 5

Put in oven for 15 to 20 minutes. Remove form the oven and pour syrup all over. Allow to cool before eating.
