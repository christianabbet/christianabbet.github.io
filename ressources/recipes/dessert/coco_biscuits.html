Coco Biscuits

8 30 min 10 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
200 g Grated coconut
185 g Flour
150 g Brown sugar
10 g Vanilla sugar
2 Egg
Sesame seed

Step 1

Pour grated coconut in a bowl. Mix it with the sugar and eggs. Add the flour and beat the dough. If it's too dry you can add some water.

Step 2

Make a ball and put it in the fridge. Wait 1 hour.

Step 3

Roll the pastry on a work surface. Dough should be 0.635cm thick. Unsing a pastry cutter, cut different shapes. Roll them in sesame seeds.

Step 4

Put in the oven in 200Â°C during 10 minutes
