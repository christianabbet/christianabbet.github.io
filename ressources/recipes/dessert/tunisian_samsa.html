Tunisian Samsa

12 45 min 20 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
8 Sheets brick pastry
400 g Almonds
250 g Sugar
2 tbsp Butter
2 tbsp Water of orange flowers
1 tbsp Cinnamon
Honey
Sesame seed

Step 1

Roasted the almonds and grind them with the sugar. Set aside

Step 2

Add the cinnamon, water of orange flowers and melted butter. Stir by hand

Step 3

Form 16 balls of dough. Set aside

Step 4

Cut the sheets in half. Butter each half sheets. Fold the sheets with a ball inside, in a triangle way

Step 5

Put the samsa in the oven in 180Â° during 20 minutes.

Step 6

Melt honey in a sauce pan. Soak samsas one after the other in the honey and drain them.

Step 7

Sprinkle sesame seeds
