French Fritters

8 20 min 20 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
300 g Flour
80 g Butter
4 tbsp Sugar
2 tbsp Kirsh
20 g Vanilla Sugar
7 g Dry Yeast
Powdered Sugar
Cinnamon
Salt

Step 1

In a bowl mix flour, dry yeast, eggs, vanilla sugar, melted butter, Kirch and a pinch salt. Knead.

Step 2

Roll out the dough and cut it into small diamond shapes.

Step 3

Fill a pan with oil and warm it. Fry the dough pieces, then drain them. Add icing sugar and cinnamon on top of it for more taste
