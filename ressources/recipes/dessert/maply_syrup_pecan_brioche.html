Maply Syrup Pecan Brioche

6 3 h 25 min

Ingredients

Dough

Quantity Name
580 g Flour
310 ml Milk
75 g Brown sugar
40 g Butter
30 g Oatmeal
14 g Dry yeast
3 Vanilla beans
2 tsp Zests of lemon
1.5 tsp Salt
1 Egg
Oil

Syrup

Quantity Name
250 ml Maple syrup
220 g Brown sugar
130 g Butter
65 g Pecans

Filling

Quantity Name
110 g Brown sugar
100 g Pecans
75 g Raisins
2 tsp Cinnamon

Step 1

Mix the oatmeal with the butter, sugar, zests of lemon and salt. In a saucepan heat the milk and pour it in the bowl to dissolve the butter and the sugar. Let cool ( Less than 40°C).

Step 2

Add the egg and the vanilla. Add 2/3 of the flour and the dry yeast. Knead. Let rest for 10 minutes.

Step 3

Gradually adding the remaining flour while mixing. When the dough is not sticky anymore form a ball. Let it rest in a bowl during 1h30-1h45. Put a linen on it. Make the syrup meanwhile.

Step 4

In a sauce pan, mix the maple syrup, butter and sugar. Mix over low heat until the sugar has dissolved.

Step 5

Pour the syrup in an bowl and sprinkle the coarsely chopped pecans. Put it in the fridge

Step 6

In a blender put the remaining pecan, raisins, sugar and cinnamon. Set aside.

Step 7

Roll the pastry. Make a rectangle of 40cm x 20cm. Drizzle with melted butter and then with the filling. Leave part of the dough on the long edge free of filling (about 2.54cm thick). Roll the dough form one side to the other and pinch to seal

Step 8

Cut slices 2.54cm thick. On a plat pour the syrup and put the slices on it and press. Let rest for 1h15 in the fridge

Step 9

Preheat the oven at 190°C. Let gild (about 12 minutes). Remove from the oven and turn all slices. Also coat the slices with some syrup using a spoon. Return to the oven for 12-15 minutes.
