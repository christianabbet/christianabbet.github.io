Hazelnuts Biscotti

8 20 min 30 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
350 g Flour
200 g Sugar
160 g Butter
100 g Hazelnuts
2 Egg
1.5 tsp Vanilla extract
1 tsp Cinnamon
0.75 tsp Baking powder
0.5 tsp Salt

Step 1

Preheat oven to 175Â°C. Grease a cookie sheet or line with parchment paper.

Step 2

In a medium bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs and vanilla. Sift together the flour, cinnamon, baking powder, and salt; mix into the egg mixture. Stir in the hazelnuts.

Step 3

Shape dough into two equal logs approximately 30cm long. Place logs on baking sheet, and flatten out to about 1cm thickness.

Step 4

Bake for about 30 minutes in preheated oven. Remove from oven to cool on the pans. When loaves are cool enough to handle, use a serrated knife to slice the loaves diagonally into 1cm thick slices. Return the slices to the baking sheet.

Step 5

Bake for an additional 10 minutes, turning over once.
