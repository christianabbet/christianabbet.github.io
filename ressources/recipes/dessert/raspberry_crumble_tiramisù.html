Raspberry Crumble Tiramisù

4 1 h 30 min

Ingredients

Tiramisù

Quantity Name
400 g Raspberries
250 g Mascarpone
5 tbsp Sugar
3 Vanilla bean
2 Egg

Crumble

Quantity Name
90 g Flour
70 g Butter
70 g Sugar
0.5 tsp Salt

Step 1

Put the flour and sugar in a bowl, then add the butter. Stir butter in by hand.

Step 2

Spread it on a plate on put in the oven in 210°C during 30 minutes (it should turn golden). Mix half-way point. Allow to cool.

Step 3

Mix the egg yolks, sugar and vanilla sugar with a rotary beater, until it whitens.

Step 4

Add the mascarpone. Mix

Step 5

Take up whites in snow with salt, and add it to the mascarpone.

Step 6

Take 4 jars, and alternate one layer of raspberries, one layer of mascarpone, one layer of crumble.

Step 7

End with a layer of crumble and put 3 full raspberries on top. Put 2h in the freezer before eating it.
