Apple Crumble

6 15 min 30 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
125 g Butter
90 g Flour
140 g Brown sugar
1 tbsp Cinnamon
5 Apple

Step 1

Preheat the oven at 180Â°C.

Step 2

Peel and dice the apples. Put them at the bottom of the mold.

Step 3

In a bowl mix the butter, flour, sugar and cinnamon.

Step 4

Sprinkle the dough on the apples.

Step 5

Put in oven during 30 minutes until the crumble is golden brown.
