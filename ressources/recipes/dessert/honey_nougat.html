Honey Nougat

12 20 min 20 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
500 g Sugar
175 g Honey
75 g Almonds
75 g Hazelnuts
1.5 dl Water
2 Egg white

Step 1

In a sauce pan mix sugar, honey and water. Het it while stirring with a plastic spatula.

Step 2

While the syrup is heating, roast the almonds and the hazelnuts

Step 3

Just before the syrup reaches 143°C take up whites in snow.

Step 4

When it reaches 143°C, slowly add the syrup in the bowl with the eggs whites while mixing it using a rotary beater (slow speed).

Step 5

Speed up the rotary beater until it thickens. The sugar will cook the egg white. Stop the rotary beater when it stops flowing.

Step 6

Add the almond and the hazelnuts. Mix.

Step 7

Pour it a mold and smooth it. Put in the fridge for 3 hours.
