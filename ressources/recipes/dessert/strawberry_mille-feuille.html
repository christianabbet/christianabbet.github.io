Strawberry Mille-Feuille

6 15 min 25 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
500 ml Milk
500 g Strawberries
75 g Sugar
20 g Butter
3 Egg yolk
1 Puff pastry
4 tbsp Icing sugar
1 tbsp Flour

Step 1

Pour the milk in a saucepan and bring to a boil.

Step 2

Meanwhile, whip the egg yolks with the sugar until it whitens. Add the flour and whip again. Add a ladle of scalding milk whipping it.

Step 3

Pour all the contents in the saucepan with the milk. Heat gently while whipping until the sauce thickens. Pour in a bowl and allow to cool.

Step 4

Preheat the oven at 180Â°C.

Step 5

Cut the puff pastry into 3 strips, put it on wax paper and butter it. Using a fork, make holes in the dough so it will not expand. Sprinkle icing sugar

Step 6

Bake for 15 minutes

Step 7

Wash, top and tail the strawberries and slice them. Then stack puff pastry, then pastry cream, then strawberries. Do it again and add the final puff pastry on top. Add some icing sugar
