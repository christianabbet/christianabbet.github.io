Appel Strudel

10 30 min 40 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
3 Apple
220 g Brown sugar
50 g Raisins
50 g Pecans
60 ml Milk
1 Puff pastry
1 Egg

Step 1

Preheat oven to 200Â°C.

Step 2

Place coarsely chopped apples in a large bowl. Stir in brown sugar and golden raisins and chopped pecans; set aside.

Step 3

Place puff pastry on baking sheet. Roll lightly with a rolling pin. Arrange apple filling down the middle of the pastry lengthwise. Fold the pastry lengthwise around the mixture. Seal edges of pastry by using a bit of water on your fingers, and rubbing the pastry edges together.

Step 4

Whisk egg and milk together, and brush onto top of pastry. Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes, or until golden brown.
