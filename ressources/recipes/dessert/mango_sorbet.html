Mango Sorbet

6 15 min -

Ingredients

Sorbet

Quantity Name
1 kg Mango
150 g Powdered sugar
100 ml Water
0.5 Lemon

Topping

Quantity Name
Gooseberries
Mint

Step 1

Peel and dice mango. Blend mango, powdered sugar, water and lemon juice.

Step 2

Put in freezer for 2 hours.

Step 3

Serve with mint leaves and gooseberries on top.
