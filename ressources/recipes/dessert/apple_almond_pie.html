Apple Almond Pie

8 20 min 30 min

Ingredients

Filling

Quantity Name
250 ml Milk
120 g Sugar
35 g Cornstarch
4 Apple
3 Egg yolk
1 Shortcrust pastry
2 tsp Cinnamon

Topping

Quantity Name
60 g Slivered almonds
20 g Butter
3 tbsp Sugar

Step 1

Preheat oven to 180Â°C.

Step 2

Peel and seed the apples. Thinly slice them and put them on the dough.

Step 3

In a bowl, mix the egg yolks with the sugar, milk, cornstarch and cinnamon. Whip. Pour over the apples.

Step 4

Bake for 30 to 35 minutes.

Step 5

Melt the butter in a small pan. Add the sugar, then the almonds and let heat while stirring. It should becomes slightly brown.

Step 6

Immediately spread over the cake.
