Peach Cobbler

8 20 min 30 min

Ingredients

Base

Quantity Name
8 Peach
50 g Sugar
55 g Brown sugar
2 tsp Cornstarch
1 tsp Lemon juice
0.25 tsp Cinnamon
0.125 tsp Nutmeg

Dough

Quantity Name
140 g Flour
50 g Sugar
55 g Brown sugar
6 tbsp Butter
1 tsp Baking powder
0.5 tsp Salt
60 ml Water

Topping

Quantity Name
3 tbsp Sugar
1 tsp Cinnamon

Step 1

Preheat oven to 220Â°C

Step 2

In a large bowl, combine sliced peaches, white sugar, brown sugar, teaspoon cinnamon, nutmeg, lemon juice, and cornstarch.

Step 3

Toss to coat evenly, and pour into a baking dish. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes.

Step 4

Meanwhile, in a large bowl, combine flour, white sugar, cup brown sugar, baking powder, and salt. Blend in butter with your fingertips, or a pastry blender, until mixture resembles coarse meal. Stir in water until just combined.

Step 5

Remove peaches from oven, and drop spoonfuls of topping over them. Sprinkle entire cobbler with the sugar and cinnamon mixture. Bake until topping is golden, about 20 minutes.
