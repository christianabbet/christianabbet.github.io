Mango Tart Tatin

6 15 min 25 min

Ingredients

Overall

Quantity Name
125 g Icing sugar
2 Lime
2 Mango
1 Shortcrust pastry

Step 1

Peel the mangoes and thinly slice them. Set aside. Put the baking paper in a mold.

Step 2

Preheat the oven at 200Â°C.

Step 3

In a sauce pan, melt the sugar to get caramel. Pour it in the mold, then spread lime zest and mangoes on it. Finally put the shortcrust pastry on the top. Make little holes in the pastry and bake for 25 minutes.

Step 4

Backward the mold (be careful the caramel flows). Let rest for 10 minutes
