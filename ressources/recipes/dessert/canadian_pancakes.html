Canadian Pancakes

4 15 min 30 min

Ingredients

Pancakes

Quantity Name
300 g Flour
90 g Sugar
30 g Butter
3 dl Milk
3 Egg
2 tsp Kirsch
7 g Dry yeast
0.25 tsp Salt

Topping

Quantity Name
Maple syrup
Strawberry
Red berries

Step 1

In a bowl put the flour, sugar, salt and yeast. Mix.

Step 2

Separate the yolks from the whites. Take them up in snow (keep the egg yolk aside) and melt the butter.

Step 3

Slowly add half of the milk and egg yolk. Hardly whip to avoid lumps.

Step 4

Slowly add the remaining milk and the melted butter. Then slowly add the egg whites, and the alcohol.

Step 5

Put in the fridge during 30 to 40 minutes

Step 6

Heat the pan and butter it
