Blueberry Muffins

8 25 min 20 min

Ingredients

Muffin

Quantity Name
190 g Flour
165 g Sugar
150 g Blueberries
80 ml Vegetable oil
80 ml Milk
1 Egg
2 tsp Baking powder
0.5 tsp Salt

Topping

Quantity Name
110 g Sugar
45 g Flour
55 g Butter
1.5 tsp Cinnamon

Step 1

Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Grease muffin cups or line with muffin liners.

Step 2

In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt and baking powder. In a bowl mix vegetable oil, egg and milk. Mix this with flour mixture. Fold in blueberries. Fill muffin cups.

Step 3

In a large bowl mix together sugar, flour, butter, and cinnamon. Mix with fork, and sprinkle over muffins before baking.

Step 4

Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until done.
